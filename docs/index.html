üí°The Spark Process

Submit new ideas and concepts through our Spark system:

  1. Submit a Spark - Use the Spark template
  2. Triage - Sparks are reviewed and categorized
  3. Discussion - Community discusses viability and approach
  4. Promotion - Approved sparks become features
  5. Implementation - Work begins through issues and PRs
